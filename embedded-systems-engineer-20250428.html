Embedded systems engineer

Munich, Germany
28.04.2025

Linque is a deep-tech start-up on the mission to develop cloud and AI infrastructure solutions for next-generation algorithms and computing. Our hardware solutions use photonic chips to achieve improvements in the energy consumption and speed of computations in comparison to current state-of-the-art hardware.

Tasks

  • Development and realization of design concepts for embedded systems with custom photonic and electronic integrated circuits.
  • Integration of low-level digital hardware such as FPGAs and microcontrollers.
  • Design and investigation of analog and mixed-signal ICs; including high-speed RF design.
  • Collaborate with our software team for efficient integration into existing tools and infrastructure.
  • Perform technical analysis and lead investigations on system design issues.
  • Contribute to product roadmaps, establish best practices in design and testing.

Your skills

  • Master’s degree or PhD in Electrical Engineering, Computer Engineering, Embedded Systems, or a related field.
  • Experience with simulation and design of analog and mixed-signal circuits (e.g. CST Studio and KLayout).
  • Prior knowledge in circuit board design (e.g. KiCad).
  • Proficiency in programming languages (Python, etc.).

We are looking at your motivation and potential more than your skills, feel free to apply also if you match only parts of the listed skills.

What we offer

  • Opportunity to join a diverse, ambitious and fast-growing team at an early stage.
  • Possibility for growth and ownership in the product and the company.
  • Ability to develop independently while still working in a highly collaborative environment.
  • Gain deep insight into the exciting topics around photonic hardware and work in cross-disciplinary teams.

If you are eager to contribute to the future of computing hardware, please reach out to set up a first discussion! We are an equal opportunity employer committed to fostering an inclusive and diverse environment.
