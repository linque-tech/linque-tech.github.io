Photonics Design engineer

Munich, Germany
28.04.2025

Linque is a deep-tech start-up on a mission to develop cloud and AI infrastructure solutions for next-generation algorithms and computing. Our hardware solutions use photonic chips to achieve improvements in the energy consumption and speed of computations in comparison to current state-of-the-art hardware.

 

Tasks

  • Design and development of schematic and layout for large-scale photonic integrated circuits.
  • Contribute to and lead the photonic design process from conception to foundry tape-out.
  • Develop, simulate and optimize design for on-chip passive and active components.
  • Collaborate with internal (product, electronics, testing) & external stakeholders (foundries, OSAT partners) to develop application-specific circuits.
  • Validate the design by designing experiments, conducting preliminary tests, and analyzing data.

 

Your skills

We focus on motivation and potential more than your skills, to promote people with diverse backgrounds to apply and find their perfect fit.

  • Master’s degree or PhD in Photonics, Physics, Electrical Engineering, or a related field.
  • Prior knowledge in PIC design and EDA tools.
  • Experience with electromagnetic and multi-physics simulations (e.g. Lumerical, COMSOL).
  • Proficiency in optical system design and analysis.

 

What we offer

  • Opportunity to join a diverse, ambitious and well-funded team at an early stage.
  • Possibility for growth and ownership in the product and the company.
  • Ability to develop independently while still working in a highly collaborative environment.
  • Learn about exciting new topics and work in cross-disciplinary teams.

If this sounds interesting, please reach out to set up a first discussion! We are an equal opportunity employer committed to fostering an inclusive and diverse environment.
