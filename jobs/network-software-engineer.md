Linque is a deep-tech start-up on the mission to develop cloud and AI infrastructure solutions for next-generation algorithms and computing. Our hardware solutions use photonic chips to achieve improvements in the energy consumption and speed of computations in comparison to current state-of-the-art hardware.

## Tasks

- Design, develop and implement a software stack and solutions for photonic hardware.
- Develop and modify APIs and the network operating system.
- Design, develop and implement software solutions for network infrastructure, focusing on improving performance, security, and reliability.
- Design test topologies to validate functionality, performance, stability and scalability of features and products.
- Collaborate with the hardware and product team to integrate tools and customer requirements with existing infrastructure.

## Your Skills

- Bachelor's or Master's degree in Computer Science, Communications Engineering, Electrical Engineering, or a related field.
- Prior knowledge in routing and switching protocols (TCP/IP, Ethernet), network operating systems (SONiC), Software Defined Networks (e.g. OVS/OVN, Calico) and network security technologies.
- Proficiency in Linux systems.
- Proficiency in scripting and programming languages such as C/C++, Go, Python and Bash.

We are looking at your motivation and potential more than your skills — feel free to apply even if you match only parts of the listed skills.

- Opportunity to join a diverse, ambitious and fast-growing team at an early stage.
- Possibility for growth and ownership in the product and the company.
- Ability to develop independently while still working in a highly collaborative environment.
- Gain deep insight into the exciting topics around photonic hardware and work in cross-disciplinary teams.
