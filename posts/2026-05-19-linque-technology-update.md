## Silicon Photonics Platform Progress

Our engineering team has achieved significant milestones in the integration of photonic circuits into standard CMOS manufacturing processes. This breakthrough enables us to deliver on our core promise: energy-efficient, high-bandwidth data processing for AI and cloud infrastructure.

## What This Means for AI Infrastructure

The demands placed on modern AI systems continue to grow exponentially. Traditional electronic interconnects struggle to keep pace with the bandwidth and latency requirements of next-generation AI models. Photonic integration offers a path forward — moving data at the speed of light while dramatically reducing power consumption.
