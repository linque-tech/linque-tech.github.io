Linque has been named a winner of the 4th Batch of the IC Taiwan Grand Challenge (ICTGC), a premier global innovation competition focused on accelerating breakthrough semiconductor and deep technology solutions organized by Taiwan's National Science and Technology Council (NSTC).

"We are grateful and humbled by this recognition by the challenge jury. This is an important and exciting step in our journey towards large-scale programmable photonics solutions.”, said Dr. Samarth Vadia, Linque CEO and Co-Founder. „Our team is looking forward to expanding our footprint and collaborations in Taiwanese ecosystem and work with the leading companies towards building a robust and scalable silicon photonics value-chain. With Linque’s disruptive silicon photonics technology, the solutions to tackle underlying bottlenecks of interconnect and memory wall and enable energy efficiency gains and latency for increasing AI workloads."
"Batch 4 attracted 209 international startups from 38 countries, said Chen Ping-Hao, Startup Marketing Manager of the ICTGC team. "A total of 11 winning teams were selected, demonstrating ICTGC's strong appeal as an international innovation platform. The winners will showcase and present at InnoVEX, Asia's largest and most iconic startup exhibition, and receive their awards during InnoVEX 2026 from June 2 to 5 in Taipei, Taiwan."

Linque’s participation in the challenge showcased the company’s differentiated approach, technical capabilities, and long-term vision for enabling innovation across the semiconductor and advanced technology landscape.

The award further strengthens Linque’s position as an emerging leader within the global photonics innovation ecosystem and opens new opportunities for strategic partnerships, research collaboration, and market expansion in Taiwan and internationally. 
Linque will take part in the ICTGC Award Ceremony & Award-winning Technologies Unveil and the InnoVEX 2026 Exposition in Taipei, Taiwan on June 2 -5. The expo will be held in conjunction with Computex at Taipei Nangang Exhibition Center, Taipei 11568, Taiwan.

## About Linque

Linque is a deep-tech company focused on delivering AI infrastructure solutions based on programmable silicon photonics technology. The company has developed low-loss photonic fabric optimized for information processing on chip by combining advanced semiconductor materials with scalable manufacturing to enable energy-efficient hardware for future computing workloads. Our first product in all-optical networking, Linque RISE, is currently developed and deployed with partners across supply-chain and industry verticals to accelerate AI training and inference workloads.
