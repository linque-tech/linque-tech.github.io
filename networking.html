Linque Rise

Linque RISE is an all-optical circuit switch to reduce the power consumption of data routing workloads. Linque RISE leverages proprietary photonic integrated circuit technology to provide high data throughput without an additional OEO conversion overhead.

Key Facts:

  • Port density: 32
  • Throughput: 12.8 Tbps
  • TDP: 50 W

Use cases: AI data centers, HPC environment

Intrinsic Network Monitoring

Benefits

  • Lower latency. Reducing end-to-end latencies towards nano- to microseconds.
  • Power consumption. Decreasing power consumption per channel to enable ‘Green Ethernet for the Enterprises’.
  • Bandwidth throughput. Accelerated optical modulation speeds boost the total switching bandwidth beyond Tbps.
  • Security. Inherent data protection and reliability due to native domain switching and processing.
  • Multiplexing. Enabling wavelength and spatial multiplexing to achieve unparalleled data throughput.
  • All-optical signal processing. Empowering signal processing without additional O-E-O conversions, to monitor and improve networks with AI methods.
